Our History



PrimeCare Dental was founded by three dentists with a passion for health care and an innate desire to serve local communities. Dr Amirul, Dr Wong and Dr Kenneth began their vision in 2015 when they acquired their first practice: L&M Dental Clinic. They vowed to create a flagship practice for the local community. Their dedication, drive and commitment was relentless.

Feeling the drive to expand their vision, reach out to more local communities around Malaysia and deliver quality dental treatment at affordable prices, the trio brought Dr Carmen, Dr Yee, Dr Sylvia & other partners on board into the team. Over the years, the group has continued to grow and have expanded to further 17 dental clinics around Kuala Lumpur, Selangor, Sabah and Penang (namely Primecare Dental Clinic, L&M Dental Clinic & Phi Dental Clinic).

The journey to strive to provide such care has been fraught with various challenges and each day the drive and determination is to better our services, nurture the staff, to treasure the relationships with our loyal patients, to humbly receive feedback and to reinvest time and energy into providing excellent oral health care.

The dream continues.
